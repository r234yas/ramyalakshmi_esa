Digitising gas stoves








This gas stove includes enhannced child-safety using the finger-print activated lock system, and smooth flame control with a digital touch screen interface. The preset-timer is a steadfast companion to keep the cooking time for you, and even regulate flame with presets. The power button is a clear signifier for the status of the stove.
